Fruits Basket

After a family tragedy turns her life upside down, plucky high schooler Tohru Honda takes matters into her own hands and moves out...into a tent! Unfortunately for her, she pitches her new home on private land belonging to the mysterious Sohma clan, and it isn't long before the owners discover her secret.

But, as Tohru quickly finds out when the family offers to take her in, the Sohmas have a secret of their own--when touched by the opposite sex, they turn into the animals of the Chinese Zodiac! But this curse has a history of abuse, isolation, ostracization, fear, and pain behind it, which has caused much suffering and broken many Sohmas.

Despite this, Tohru may just be able to heal the Sohmas hearts and soothe their souls, and even break the curse once and for all.

There are two versions of this show - the first one being made in 2001 and the second and more popular being made in 2019 - 2022. The first one ran for only 26 episodes whereas the second version ran for 4 seasons. There is also a prelude of everything that happened before the show, as well as a manga.

They are available to stream on Crunchyroll, Funimation, and Hulu.
